Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg (OVGU) - Institutional Overview
Basic Information
Official Name

German: Otto-von-Guericke-Universität Magdeburg
English: Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg
Abbreviation: OVGU

Key Facts

Type: Public research university
Founded: 1993 (one of Germany's youngest universities)
Location: Magdeburg, capital city of Saxony-Anhalt, Germany
Student Population: Approximately 13,000 students
Number of Faculties: 9

Namesake
The university is named after Otto von Guericke, physicist and former mayor of Magdeburg, known for his pioneering work on vacuum technology and atmospheric pressure.

Historical Background
Foundation

Established: 1993 through merger of three predecessor institutions
Predecessor Institutions:

Technical University
University of Education
Medical Academy



Campus Structure
Due to the three predecessor institutions, OVGU maintains three distinct campuses:

Universitätsplatz - Main campus
Universitätsklinikum - Medical campus/University Hospital
Zschokkestrasse - Additional campus location

European Integration

Since 2022: Member of "EU Green" European university network
Network Size: Nine universities total
Purpose: Promote international cooperation to address future social challenges


Academic Structure
Nine Faculties

Faculty of Computer Science (FIN)

Focus: Information technology, software engineering, data science
Relevant to IMIQ: AI, machine learning, autonomous systems


Faculty of Economics and Management (FWW)

Focus: Business administration, economics, management sciences
Relevant to IMIQ: Mobility business models, transport economics


Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences and Education (FHW)

Focus: Social sciences, education, cultural studies
Relevant to IMIQ: Environmental psychology (Prof. Ellen Matthies), user behavior


Faculty of Mathematics (FMA)

Focus: Pure and applied mathematics, statistics
Relevant to IMIQ: Optimization, modeling, simulation algorithms


Faculty of Medicine (FME)

Focus: Medical research, clinical practice, health sciences
Campus: Universitätsklinikum (University Hospital)


Faculty of Natural Sciences (FNW)

Focus: Physics, chemistry, biology
Relevant to IMIQ: Sensor technology, environmental monitoring


Faculty of Mechanical Engineering (FMB)

Focus: Mechanical systems, automotive engineering, robotics
Relevant to IMIQ: Vehicle technology, autonomous systems


Faculty of Process and Systems Engineering (FVST)

Focus: Process engineering, systems engineering, logistics
Relevant to IMIQ: Logistics systems (Prof. Hartmut Zadek), supply chain


Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT)

Focus: Electrical engineering, automation, communication technology
Relevant to IMIQ: Cognitive systems (Prof. Andreas Wendemuth), sensor systems, V2X communication
